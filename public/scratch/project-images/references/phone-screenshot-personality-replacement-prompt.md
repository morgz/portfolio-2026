# Phone Screenshot Replacement + App Personality Prompt

Use the supplied hand-held phone reference image as the base composition. Replace only the visible phone screen content with the supplied app screenshot.

Preserve the original phone case, pose, lighting, camera angle, crop, background color, shadows, reflections, and overall photographic style. Fit the screenshot naturally onto the phone display so it follows the exact screen orientation, perspective, curvature, scale, and edge alignment of the original device. The inserted screenshot should look like it was genuinely photographed on the phone, with believable brightness, contrast, glare, screen glass reflection, subtle blur, and any perspective distortion required by the angle.

Vary the person holding the phone from the base reference by choosing a different ethnicity and gender presentation for the hand. Keep the hand realistic, naturally posed, and integrated into the original composition, with believable skin texture, anatomy, lighting, shadows, and contact with the phone.

Interpret the personality, category, and visual language of the supplied app screenshot, then express that personality subtly through the person holding the phone. Add small, tasteful styling details that feel conceptually connected to the app, such as the cuff fabric, sleeve color, jewelry, nail color, rings, bracelet, small tattoo, charm, or texture. These details should feel like wardrobe and styling choices made for a campaign shoot, not obvious props or literal icons.

Keep the personality details refined and understated. They should support the concept of the app without distracting from the phone. Do not cover the screen, change the core hand pose, change the phone case shape, alter the background, or make the image look composited.

Avoid adding text outside the phone screen. Avoid logos, mascots, floating UI, stickers, labels, extra devices, heavy costume changes, exaggerated accessories, or anything that makes the hand styling feel gimmicky. The final image should feel like a polished editorial product mockup: realistic, stylish, bright, tactile, and ready for a portfolio or app launch image.

## Inputs

- Base image: one of the handheld phone reference images.
- Screen image: the app screenshot to place on the phone.

## Output Goal

A realistic edited image where the phone displays the supplied screenshot in the correct perspective, the hand uses a different ethnicity and gender presentation from the base reference, and the hand styling quietly reflects the app's personality.
